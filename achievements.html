ACHIEVEMENTS

Keshi's career is home to many successes and accomplishments. Here are a few:

  • GABRIEL, keshi's debut album, earning Top New Artist Debut of 2022, a top 5 spot on Billboard Top Album Sales charts, and a top 20 on Billboard's Top 200
  • Having over 2.5B streams on Spotify
  • HELL/HEAVEN, one of keshi's tours, selling out during pre-sales
  • Contributed to the discography of Marvel's Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings, with his song War With Heaven
  • Skeletons, one of keshi's EPs, landing 50th on Billboard Top Album Sales charts and landing 5th on The Heatseekers charts.

Of course, keshi has many more successes in his career.
